Doyi Kim

I am a Ph.D. student at KAIST, advised by Prof. Changick Kim.

I work on data-driven modeling of climate systems, with a focus on linking observation, prediction, and impact. My research integrates diverse observational data to model extreme events and their downstream risks, moving beyond forecasting toward decision-relevant climate intelligence. I am particularly interested in quantifying climate adaptation under uncertainty, incorporating economic perspectives into AI-driven climate risk analysis.
