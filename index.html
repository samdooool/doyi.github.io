Doyi Kim

I am a Ph.D. student at the Graduate School of Green Growth and Sustainability, KAIST, advised by Prof. Changick Kim. Previously, I received my M.S. in Climate and Energy System Engineering and my B.S. in Environmental Science and Engineering from Ewha Womans University.

My research lies at the intersection of AI, Earth observation, and climate adaptation. I work on satellite and deep learning based weather forecasting, extreme hazard and impact profiling, and decision value metrics for climate adaptation under uncertainty. My research has been shaped by firsthand experience working in data-sparse regions, where improving forecasting and early warning systems can directly support real-world decisions.
